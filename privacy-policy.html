Privacy Policy — Ayurvani

Last updated: July 2026

Ayurvani ("the app", "the website") is an educational reference app for Ayurvedic texts (Charaka Samhita, Dravya Guna materia medica, and related content). This page explains what information the app does and does not collect.

What we collect

Ayurvani does not require you to create an account, and does not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifiers to use the app.

  • On-device storage only: Favorites you save, language preference (Hindi/English), and light/dark theme choice are stored locally on your own device (browser local storage). This data is never transmitted to us and stays only on your device unless you clear your browser/app data.
  • No analytics or advertising trackers: The app does not use third-party advertising SDKs or behavioral analytics/tracking services.

Search features

When you use the Caraka Finder, shloka search, or Dravya Guna search features, your search text is sent to our server solely to look up and return the matching result to you. Search queries are not linked to your identity and are not stored or sold.

Third-party content sources

Some reference text is retrieved from public Ayurvedic text sources (such as carakasamhitaonline.com) to display verses and commentary within the app. Fetching this content may involve a request to that third-party server; no personal data about you is sent as part of that request beyond what's technically necessary to retrieve the page (e.g. standard network request headers).

Admin panel

Ayurvani includes a password-protected admin panel used only by the app's maintainers to manage content. This is not a visitor account system and does not collect visitor personal data.

Children's privacy

Ayurvani is an educational reference tool suitable for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.
