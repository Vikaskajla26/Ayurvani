## What the project involves

We are creating a dataset of traditional Ayurvedic shloka recitations (Charaka Samhita,
Sushruta Samhita, and Ashtanga Hridayam) to build an open-source pronunciation study tool
for BAMS students. The tool will help students learn correct Sanskrit prosody and articulation
for their professional examinations.

Your recorded voice will be used to:
1. Train a text-to-speech (TTS) model that generates reference recitations of Ayurvedic shlokas
2. Power a pronunciation scoring module where students compare their recitation to yours
3. Potentially appear in audio demonstrations on the project website or publications

## What we are asking of you

- Participate in **3–5 recording sessions** of approximately 1 hour each
- Recite specified shlokas from the Charaka/Sushruta/Ashtanga Hridaya as directed by the protocol
- Allow audio recordings to be used for the purposes described above
